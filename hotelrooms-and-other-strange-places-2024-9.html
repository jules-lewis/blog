    Hotelrooms and Other Strange Places 2024: #9

    February 15, 2025 Jules
    CTF OSINT

    The flag is in sight. Or in Warsaw, in fact…

    This is a walkthrough of an OSINT CTF set by The SEINT in a GitHub repo you can find here. You can see how the challenge works, and how I solved Step 1 at the start of the series.

    SPOILER ALERT!

If you want to have a go at solving The SEINT's puzzle yourself, please DON'T read on here, as this is obviously heavily spoilered.

    Step 9

    This is the final puzzle of the series, and we’re back to simple text:

    It’s December now and we are all back home from our adventures around the world. How did I know that even my friends are home? They sent me an invitation to meet for a nice winter cup of hot coffee. The meeting place was of course not sent directly. That would be too easy. Instead, they sent me a short message, which contained just a couple of words and said that our meeting point would be the place: only.sector.deduced. Where can that be? Can you help me?

    Find the building there and the year that building was built. The hash of the year and the city name written in lowercase (like this: 1900london) is the answer.

    Hopefully, most people now recognise that three words connected by dots as a geographical reference means we’re turning to What3Words.

    This gets us to this modern building in Warsaw, adjacent to the historic boundary of the Warsaw Ghetto (depicted in the featured image):

    A search on the address shows the development is referred to as Park Place 3. The English version of this site appears to be broken, but the site can be translated well enough by my browser. This then reveals the information we’re after on one of the tiles:

    There now follows a bit of trial and error, because there are three potential years. You will get there much quicker if you’re following the instructions and not putting the city before the year, like I did!

    And, with the feeling of anticlimax I get at the end of all CTF’s, I’m rewarded with The Seint’s token and realise I’m at the end of the journey:

    Whatever am I going to do next?
